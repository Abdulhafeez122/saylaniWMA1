Biryani Masala is a spice blend used to prepare delicious and aromatic biryani, a popular South Asian rice dish. It contains a mix of flavorful spices like coriander, cumin, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, black pepper, bay leaves, nutmeg, and red chili powder. Some blends may also include ginger, garlic, and dried herbs. Biryani Masala adds rich flavor, color, and aroma to the dish. It is used while cooking meat, rice, and gravy to enhance the overall taste. It is available as a ready-made powder in stores, or it can be made at home using whole spices.

Sugar is a sweet-tasting substance commonly used in foods and drinks. It is a carbohydrate that provides the body with energy. Sugar is naturally found in fruits (as fructose) and milk (as lactose), but the most common type used in cooking is sucrose, which comes from sugarcane or sugar beets. Sugar is used to sweeten food, preserve jams, and add flavor to baked goods. While it adds taste and energy, too much sugar can lead to health problems like tooth decay, obesity, and diabetes, so it should be used in moderation.

Custard is a sweet, creamy dessert made from milk, sugar, and eggs or custard powder. It has a smooth texture and is often served as a dessert or used as a topping for cakes, fruits, and puddings. Custard can be served hot or cold, and comes in different flavors like vanilla, mango, banana, and strawberry. It is easy to prepare and is a favorite treat for both children and adults. Instant custard is also available, which is made by mixing custard powder with milk and sugar, then heating until thick.
